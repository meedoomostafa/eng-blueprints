## 1. Field Axioms (Operations)

### Addition Rules

- **(1) Associative Property:**$$(a+b)+c = a+(b+c)$$
- **(2) Identity Property:**$$a+0 = 0+a = a$$
    > [!NOTE]
    > 
    > $0$ is the additive identity.

- **(3) Inverse Property:** For every number $a$, there exists a number $-a$ such that:$$a+(-a) = (-a)+a = 0$$
- **(4) Commutative Property:**$$a+b = b+a$$

### Multiplication Rules

- **(5) Associative Property:**$$(a \times b) \times c = a \times (b \times c)$$
- **(6) Identity Property:**$$a \times 1 = 1 \times a = a$$
    > [!NOTE]
    > 
    > $1$ is the multiplicative identity.

- **(7) Inverse Property:** For every number $a$ (where $a \neq 0$), there exists a number $a^{-1}$ such that: $$a \times a^{-1} = a^{-1} \times a = 1$$
- **(8) Commutative Property:**$$a \times b = b \times a$$
### Joint Property

- **(9) Distributive Property:**$$a \times (b+c) = a \times b + a \times c$$
## 2. Order Axioms (General Rules)

- **(10) Trichotomy Law:** For every number $a$, only one of the following conditions holds true:
    - $a = 0$
    - $a$ is a positive number
    - $a$ is a negative number
- **(11) Closure under Addition:** If $a$ and $b$ are positive numbers, then $(a+b)$ is also a positive number.
- **(12) Closure under Multiplication:** If $a$ and $b$ are positive numbers, then $(a \times b)$ is also a positive number.

### Definition of Inequalities

- $a > b \iff a - b > 0$
- $a < b \iff b - a > 0$

## 3. Theorems

### Triangle Inequality

- **(1) Absolute Value Property:** For any numbers $a$ and $b$:$$|a + b| \le |a| + |b|$$
